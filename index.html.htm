Our Mission

Each year, one-third of all food produced is wasted. Our mission is to reduce food waste through education, community action, and donations to local shelters and food banks.

Tips to Reduce Food Waste

  • Plan your meals and shop with a list.
  • Store food correctly to extend shelf life.
  • Use leftovers creatively in new meals.
  • Compost food scraps when possible.

Donate or Volunteer

Support local food banks or join us in food rescue events. Your small act can feed someone in need.
